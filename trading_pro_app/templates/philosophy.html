Trading can get increasingly complex without venturing too far in which is why I aim to simplify things. If you can remember a few basic principles, you will lay a solid foundation for profitable trading. 1 & 2 are the most important and the rest are in no particular order.

1. If you aren't prepared to develop and implement a risk-management plan, then stop right now.

2. Develop and implement a risk-management system (I will explain this on the position size calculator page).

3. Create and stick to your plan. This is so crucial and is something most people have to learn the hard way.

4. Don't chase trades.

5. Ignore the noise because there is a lot of it out there. Remember that the goal isn't to catch the bottom and top of every trade. It is about slicing off a piece of the action in between the bottom and the top

6. Trade with the trend. Remember, "The trend is your friend."

7. Keep things simple. As you will learn, there are a host of indicators out there which can be employed in a strategy but it doesn't mean you need to watch more than 1 or 2. This is a great place to get lost but I would encourage you to look around at a variety of them to see what makes the most sense to you.

8. You can't predict price, so don't try to. Remember that trading is more about having a game plan at different levels should price visit that area of interest.
